Battery Disposal Guide

Used batteries should never be thrown into regular household bins.

Batteries contain hazardous chemicals such as lead, cadmium, and lithium that may contaminate soil and groundwater.

Store used batteries safely until they can be taken to an authorized battery collection center.

Rechargeable batteries should be recycled separately from normal household waste.

Damaged batteries should be handled carefully and delivered to e-waste recycling facilities.

Proper battery recycling helps recover valuable materials and reduces environmental pollution.
